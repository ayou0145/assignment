Ingredients
  • Rice Paper
  • Carrots
  • Tofu
  • Mushrooms
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Peanut Butter
  • Mirin Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar

Instructions
  1. Slice up your purple cabbage and put it in a bowl, drizzle some mirin sauce and rice vinegar on top

  2. Slice the carrots up into thin 3 cm long pieces

  3. In a pan, place some sesame oil and a garlic clove sliced up, let the garlic become golden and sizzly, then chuck in some thin pieces of tofu until they become grilled on all sides

  4. Take out the tofu and throw in your mushrooms (any kind) sliced up, chuck them in there with some oyster sauce, another drizzle of sesame oil and a splash of soy sauce,let this marinate and simmer

  5. Boil some water and cook your rice noodles, then get all your ingredients ready to use (place ingredients on small plates etc.)

  6. Inorder to prepare the rolls, simply put a piece of rice paper under some cold water so that it is completely wet, then place it on your plate, it should take 30 seconds for it to go transparent , then place your ingredients in the center of the paper and form your rolls by packing them up like a parcel
